      • I am a postdoctoral scholar at Vanderbilt University School of Nursing and a critical care nurse. I am passionate about improving care, and the health and well-being of ICU patients who are transitioning home after acute hospital care and their families. I am particularly interested in advancing our understanding of the role of ICU nurses in patient- and family-centered transitional care planning and decision-making for ICU patients and their families. My long-term goal is to develop and test patient- and family-centered ICU transitional care interventions to improve patient quality of life and decrease caregiver strain for ICU patients and their families during the transition to home after acute hospital care through research.
